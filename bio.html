About

Hello, I’m Daniel Jenkins, and I’ve been making films for as long as I can remember! What started with homemade skits and improvised sets grew into a lifelong commitment to directing and storytelling.

I’m currently double majoring in Digital Cinema (Directing) and 3D Animation & Game Development. I chose both paths intentionally. I believe strong directors understand not only story and performance, but also the technical systems that bring worlds to life.

Alongside my studies, I work two jobs, including serving as a tutor at my university, where I help students succeed in the same filmmaking and Animation courses I’m completing. Balancing academics, creative work, and professional responsibilities has shaped my discipline and reinforced my commitment to this field.

I’ve completed multiple film and game projects, both independently and as part of a team. I’m dependable, collaborative, and committed to seeing projects through.

I take this work very seriously, I'm dedicated to the craft and to the people I create alongside. My goal is to keep growing, technically, creatively, and collaboratively, and to build a career grounded in bringing stories, worlds, and characters to life!

Based in Utah. Available for collaboration
